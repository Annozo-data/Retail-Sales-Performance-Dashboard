# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail sales performance across multiple regions, product categories, and sales representatives.

The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Questions

The analysis aimed to answer the following questions:

* Which region generated the highest revenue?
* Which product category performed best?
* Who was the top-performing sales representative?
* How did sales fluctuate throughout the month?
* What patterns existed in customer ratings?

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains:

* Order ID
* Date
* Product Category
* Product
* Unit Sold
* Unit Price
* Revenue
* Region
* Sales Representative
* Customer Rating

---

## Key Insights

* Abuja recorded the highest revenue performance.
* Electronics generated over 56% of total revenue.
* David was the top-performing sales representative.
* Sales peaked on May 3 and May 8.
* Beauty products recorded the lowest revenue contribution.

---

## Recommendations

* Increase investment in Electronics inventory and promotions.
* Investigate low-performing categories and regions.
* Replicate successful sales strategies used by top performers.
* Improve customer engagement to increase average ratings.
